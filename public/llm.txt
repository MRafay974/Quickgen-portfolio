# Quickgen

> Quickgen is a full-stack product design and engineering studio specialising in hardware, firmware, PCB design, mobile apps, and IoT solutions for global clients. We take products from napkin sketch to production — design, engineer, build, ship.

## About

Quickgen is a multidisciplinary product studio. We partner with start-ups and enterprises to create physical and digital products. Our team covers the full product development lifecycle: industrial design, mechanical engineering, PCB and firmware development, mobile and web software, and manufacturing coordination.

### Services

Summary: Describes the four core service pillars offered by Quickgen.

**Design**
Tags: Product design, Branding, UX/UI, Design research, Prototyping, Modelling, Packaging
Description: Creating impactful designs that blend creativity and strategy. User-centered solutions from concept to production-ready visuals.
Example clients: AtomicForm, Veba Baby, RCMP, Otis Dental.

**Engineering**
Tags: Mechanical Design and CAD, PCB Design and Schematics, Part Analysis and Testing, Component Sourcing, Certification
Description: Hardware expertise spanning mechanical design, PCB development, and design for manufacturability (DFM). Thorough component research, analysis, and testing.

**Software**
Tags: Web Development, Mobile Apps, Embedded Firmware, Cloud & IoT, UI/UX Engineering
Description: Robust software systems from mobile apps and web platforms to embedded firmware and cloud infrastructure. Bridging hardware and software for connected product experiences.
Example clients: BreatheSuite, Velavu, Otis Dental, AVSS.

**Manufacturing**
Tags: DFM Consulting, Supplier Sourcing, Injection Moulding, PCBA, Assembly & Fulfilment
Description: Taking products from design to production — supplier relationships, quality control, and logistics. Products built to spec, on time, and at scale.
Example clients: Veba Baby, AtomicForm, Handicare, GaitTronics.
